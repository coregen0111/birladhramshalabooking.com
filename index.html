OUR STORY

Tradition of Hospitality in Ayodhya

Birla Dharamshala Ayodhya is a unit of Birla Trust, dedicated to serving guests with devotion, hospitality, and care. Located in the heart of the holy city, we provide a peaceful retreat for pilgrims and travelers alike.

With modern amenities blended with traditional values, we ensure your stay in Ayodhya is comfortable, safe, and spiritually enriching. Experience the sacred vibes of Lord Ram's city with the comfort of home.
